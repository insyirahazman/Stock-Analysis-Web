MYStocks

MYStocks is a Malaysian-based stock analysis platform that focuses on NASDAQ-listed stocks. It integrates the Twelve Data API and TradingView widgets to deliver real-time market data, visualizations, and analytic insights for traders and investors. The platform aims to provide a user-friendly, cost-free experience for beginners, students, and retail investors.
